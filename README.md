Data Collection & Understanding
The dataset includes mobile phone details such as brand, model, price, ratings, reviews, RAM, storage, battery capacity, camera specifications, and processor type.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Data analysis is performed to understand price distribution, brand-wise performance, and relationships between specifications (RAM, storage, battery) and price.

Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

Missing and inconsistent values are handled.

Text-based features (specifications) are cleaned and standardized.

Categorical variables such as brand and processor are encoded for analysis.

Feature Analysis & Visualization
Visualizations are created to compare mobile prices across brands, analyze rating trends, and identify popular configurations among customers.

Insights & Findings
Key insights are derived, such as the most popular brands, optimal price ranges, and specifications that significantly impact customer ratings and pricing.

Conclusion
The analysis helps understand market trends, customer preferences, and competitive pricing strategies in the mobile phone segment on Flipkart.
